About us
Weri Spezials is a Latvian-German company located in Riga, operating since 1994. Following centuries-old traditions, Weri Spezials produces durable and ergonomic socks and tights for people of every age. We stick to eco-friendly manufactory standards, because we care about the health of our customers and the environment
